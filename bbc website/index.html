Who We Are

Built in China.
Trusted Worldwide.

Founded in 2009 in Ganzhou, Jiangxi, BBA has established itself as an industry-leading manufacturer of high-performance BOPP and PVC films. Over the last decade and a half, we have refined our multi-layer co-extrusion technology to deliver films of absolute thickness precision and optical clarity.

Our state-of-the-art facility adheres to strict ISO 9001:2015 quality standards, deploying automated testing checks at every step. From local sourcing optimization to streamlined customs clearance, BBA manages full production lifecycles factory-direct.

We serve high-standard global buyers, including large-scale tobacco operations, cosmetic companies, and food packaging packers. For those looking for custom configurations, our team delivers custom specifications with unmatched turnaround times.
