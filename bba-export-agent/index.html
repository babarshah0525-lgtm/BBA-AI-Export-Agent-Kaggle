Our Offerings

Industrial Packaging Product Range

BOPP Packaging Film

Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene Film featuring superior mechanical, optical, and barrier properties. Ideal for food packaging, labeling, and lamination.

  • Thickness: 12 - 40 μm
  • Available Types: Plain, Matt, Pearlized, Heat Sealable
  • Key Features: High clarity, moisture barrier, excellent tensile strength

PVC Shrink Film

Polyvinyl Chloride shrink film with superb clarity and high shrinkage rates. Perfect for bottle sleeves, caps, cosmetics, and protective multi-packs.

  • Shrink Ratio: Up to 50% - 60%
  • Applications: Beverage labels, food jars, battery sleeves, cosmetics
  • Key Features: Low shrink temperature, glass-like transparency, high glossy finish

Custom Packaging Solutions

Tailor-made multi-layer laminate bags, stand-up pouches with zippers, and printed roll stocks customized to your exact moisture and oxygen barrier specs.

  • Printing: High-definition rotogravure up to 10 colors
  • Pouch Formats: Stand-up, Zipper pouch, Quad seal, Flat bottom
  • Material Blends: PET/AL/PE, PET/VMPET/PE, BOPP/CPP
